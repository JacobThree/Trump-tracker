Explanations

CPI (YoY inflation): Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers, measuring the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers. Source: FRED.

Real GDP growth: Real Gross Domestic Product, percent change from preceding period, seasonally adjusted annual rate. Source: FRED.

Unemployment rate: Unemployment Rate (U-3), representing the number of unemployed as a percentage of the labor force. Source: FRED.
